# MODE: TRAINING EVALUATOR
# Command: /career-ops training [course/cert name or URL]
# Purpose: Evaluate whether a certification or course is worth Rume's time

## Evaluation Dimensions (6)

| # | Dimension | Weight | Question |
|---|-----------|--------|---------|
| 1 | North Star alignment | 30% | Does this move Rume closer to a Lead AI/ML Engineer IC role? |
| 2 | Recruiter signal strength | 25% | Will UK enterprise recruiters recognise this cert? |
| 3 | Time/effort cost | 15% | Hours required vs value returned |
| 4 | Opportunity cost | 15% | What else could Rume do with this time? |
| 5 | Content risk | 10% | Is the content outdated, vendor-locked, or superficial? |
| 6 | Portfolio deliverable | 5% | Does completing this produce a showable project or cert? |

## Output

**Verdict: PROCEED / REJECT / CONDENSE**
- PROCEED: High value, clear ROI, do it fully
- REJECT: Low signal, high time cost, skip
- CONDENSE: Partial value — extract the key module only, don't complete in full

**Reasoning:** [2–3 sentences]
**Time estimate:** [hours]
**Alternatives to consider:** [if REJECT or CONDENSE]
